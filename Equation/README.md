# Equations 

## LaTex code and their meaning
- \quad = space equale to current font size(=18mu)

    -1em=18mu, 1em=10pt
    
- \ ,	3/18 of \quad (= 3 mu)

- \ :	4/18 of \quad (= 4 mu)

- \ ;	5/18 of \quad (= 5 mu)

- \ !	-3/18 of \quad (= -3 mu)

- \ (space after backslash!)	equivalent of space in normal text

- \qquad	twice of \quad (= 36 mu)

#Examples

- f(x) &= x^2\! +3x\! +2                    ------

- f(x) &= x^2+3x+2                          ------

-f(x) &= x^2\, +3x\, +2                     ------

-f(x) &= x^2\: +3x\: +2                     ------

-f(x) &= x^2\; +3x\; +2                     ------

-f(x) &= x^2\ +3x\ +2                       ------

-f(x) &= x^2\quad +3x\quad +2               ------

-f(x) &= x^2\qquad +3x\qquad +2             ------
